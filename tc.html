Terms & Conditions for courses held in Summer 2017.



The following courses will be conducted in April 2017 as a part of Learn Via Experiments.

The Learn Marathon.
Photography.
Robotics.


Listed below are details and instructions for the respective courses. Please read the instructions carefully.

The Learn Marathon:

A summer camp for children of ages 6-11. Children will engage in activities relating to craft, art, and scientific experiments.* Experiments based on simple concepts of science will be demonstrated to make children excited and curious about science and its applications. All materials will be provided.


*All topics covered are not optional. The camp will include all topics. In case of no desire to engage in any one of the topics, the student may still join and not participate in the specific activity.


Dates: 21st April - 1st May 2017. Everyday except Sunday.
Timings: 3pm - 5pm.
Fees: INR 1,000.


Robotics:

Basic and intermediate robotics course for children of ages 12-16. Course will contain basics of Arduino programming, introduction to CNC technology and the making of a basic miniature of CNC plotter. Necessary robotics kits will be provided which can be taken back by the students.


Dates: 21st April - 1st May 2017. Everyday except Sunday.
Timings: 4pm - 6pm.
Fees: INR 2,000 (inclusive of equipment)




Photography:
Basic and intermediate photography course for the children of ages 12-16. Course will include basic concepts of photography and lighting. Participants are expected to bring any camera along. Other equipment will be provided for the duration of the course.
Dates: 21st April - 1st May. Everyday except Sunday.
Timings: 4pm - 6pm.
Fees: INR 500.



General Instructions:

  • Parents and students are expected to attend the inauguration day on 20th April 2017 at 5:30 pm. There will be a small talk on what the programme will consist of given by a faculty member. The duration of the first day will be 30 minutes. (5:30 - 6 pm)

  • Children can participate in multiple courses if wished, if there is no issue with respect to course timings.

  • Students are expected to maintain discipline in the premises during the entire camp. Since the premises is a multi-facility office building with various offices, misbehavior and indiscipline will not be tolerated.

  • Parents are also expected to pick their ward immediately after the classes end. In case there is any delay, the students may stay in the building until they are picked up. Students will not be allowed to leave without the arrival of their respective guardians.

  • In case of any student travelling by public transport, parents may please inform us about the same for exemption from the above stated rule.

    • *Students coming for Robotics and Photography who have attended the December course are not required to bring their parents to the venue. *Students are required to complete the first stage of registration on our website lvegoa.cf
